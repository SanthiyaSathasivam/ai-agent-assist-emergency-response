# Why Now

## Problem Acceleration

Emergency systems are facing increasing pressure:

- Rising call volumes and complexity
- Increasing cognitive load on operators
- Higher expectations for speed and accuracy

## Technology Inflection Point

Recent advancements make this product viable today:

- Real-time speech-to-text with <2s latency
- Mature NLP models for intent and severity detection
- Scalable cloud infrastructure for real-time processing

## Industry Shift

- Transition from protocol-driven → intelligence-assisted decision-making
- Growing need for auditability and compliance
- Increased focus on consistency across operators and regions

## Risk of Inaction

Without intervention:

- Slower response times
- Increased operator fatigue
- Higher variability in decision quality
- Greater legal and compliance exposure

## Timing Advantage

This is the first moment where:

Technology capability + Operational need + Regulatory push  
→ converge to make real-time AI assist both feasible and necessary
