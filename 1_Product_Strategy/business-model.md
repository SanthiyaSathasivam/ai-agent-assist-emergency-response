# Business Model

## Customers

- Public safety agencies (911 systems)
- Emergency medical services (EMS)
- Private emergency response providers

## Value Proposition

- Faster response times → better outcomes
- Reduced errors → lower liability
- Improved consistency → higher compliance
- Reduced cognitive load → better operator performance

## Pricing Strategy

### SaaS Model

- Per operator / per seat pricing
- Usage-based pricing (per call volume)

### Tiered Offering

| Tier        | Features                          |
|------------|----------------------------------|
| Basic       | Real-time transcription          |
| Pro         | AI recommendations + scoring     |
| Enterprise  | Analytics + compliance + insights|

## ROI Drivers

- Reduction in decision time (25–40%)
- Improved resource allocation
- Reduced legal/compliance risk
- Increased operator efficiency

## Buyer Persona

Primary Buyer:
- Government agencies / public safety departments

Secondary Buyer:
- Private EMS operators

## Sales Motion

- Pilot → Demonstrate impact → Expand deployment
