# Return on Investment (ROI)

## Objective

Quantify the business value created by the AI-assisted decision support system for emergency response organizations.

---

# 1. Value Drivers

| Value Driver              | Description                                      | Impact Area                    |
|----------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| Faster Decision-Making     | Reduced time to dispatch                         | Operational efficiency         |
| Improved Accuracy          | Fewer misclassifications                         | Risk reduction                 |
| Increased Consistency      | Standardized decisions across operators          | Quality improvement            |
| Reduced Cognitive Load     | Lower operator fatigue                           | Productivity + retention       |
| Auditability               | Traceable decisions                              | Compliance + legal protection  |

---

# 2. Quantified Impact (Illustrative)

## A. Decision Time Reduction

| Metric                  | Before           | After            | Impact                          |
|--------------------------|------------------|------------------|----------------------------------|
| Avg Decision Time        | 30 seconds       | 18–22 seconds    | 25–40% improvement               |

---

## B. Operator Efficiency

| Metric                  | Before           | After            | Impact                          |
|--------------------------|------------------|------------------|----------------------------------|
| Calls per hour           | 80–100           | 100–120          | Increased throughput             |

---

## C. Error Reduction

| Metric                  | Before           | After            | Impact                          |
|--------------------------|------------------|------------------|----------------------------------|
| Misclassification Rate   | Baseline         | Reduced          | Lower risk and better outcomes   |

---

| Stakeholder        | Value Delivered                          |

---

# 6. Payback Consideration

The system delivers value through:

- Immediate efficiency gains (time reduction)  
- Medium-term accuracy improvements  
- Long-term risk and compliance benefits  

Adoption can be justified based on operational improvements alone, with additional upside from risk reduction.

---

# Product Implication

The product creates value by:

- Reducing time per decision at scale  
- Improving decision quality in critical scenarios  
- Standardizing performance across operators  

ROI is driven by measurable operational improvements rather than theoretical AI benefits.
